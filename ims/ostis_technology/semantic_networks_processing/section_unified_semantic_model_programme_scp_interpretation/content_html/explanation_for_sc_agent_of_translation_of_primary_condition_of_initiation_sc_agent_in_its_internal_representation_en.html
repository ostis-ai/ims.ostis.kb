Task of the sc-agent of translation of primary condition of initiation sc-agent in its internal representation is translation of the binary oriented pairs, describing primary condition of initiation of some sc-agent, in internal representation of sc-events at the level of sc-memoryimplementation. Condition of initiation of this sc-agent is appearancing in sc-memory new element of the set of active sc-agents, for which it will be found and translated the appropriate binary pair.
