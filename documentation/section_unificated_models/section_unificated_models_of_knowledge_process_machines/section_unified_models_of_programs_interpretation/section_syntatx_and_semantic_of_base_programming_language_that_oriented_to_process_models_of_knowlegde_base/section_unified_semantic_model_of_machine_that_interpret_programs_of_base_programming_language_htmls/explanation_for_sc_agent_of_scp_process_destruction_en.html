The task of the sc-agent of scp-process destruction" is destruction of an scp-process,that is deleting from sc-memory of all sc-elements, its components. This sc-agent is activated in case of appearance in sc-memory of an scp-process, belonging to the class of the useless scp-processes.

Thus destroyed scp-process shall be optionally completely created. Need of destruction not until the end of the created can arise if in case of creation scp-process can arise if in case of creation of scp-process were problems, not allowing to continue creation of scp-process and its execution.
